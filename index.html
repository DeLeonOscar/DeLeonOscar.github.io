Oscar De Leon Vazquez

I currently work at Boeing as a Manufacturing Engineer supporting the 737 Program. I hold both a bachelor’s and a master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. My academic background includes applied research using convolutional neural networks for autonomous vehicle applications. I am interested in combining data, automation, and mechanical design to solve complex engineering problems.

Outside of work, I serve as the President of the SHPE Puget Sound Professional Chapter. In this role, I lead a team of directors in creating events that promote Hispanic representation in STEM.

In my free time, you can find me playing sports—especially soccer—traveling, participating in community outreach with SHPE and Boeing, and learning new things.
